Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is a versatile and easy-to-make dish that can be customized with various sauces and ingredients. This simple pasta recipe will guide you through making a delicious meal in just a few steps.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (200g)
  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Tomato sauce (1 cup)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • Basil (fresh or dried, 1 tsp)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, ¼ cup)

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add Tomato Sauce: Pour in the tomato sauce and stir. Season with salt, pepper, and basil. Let the sauce simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Combine: Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Serve: Transfer the pasta to serving plates and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve hot.
